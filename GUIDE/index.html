Sidebar Navigation

Navigation

The left sidebar provides access to all system modules. Here's a breakdown of each section:

⚙️

Settings → Account

Manage your profile, update login credentials, and configure personal preferences.

📊

Dashboard

The main overview page with widgets for compliance reports, recent activity, archiving status, and maintenance schedules.

📂

Management

Expandable menu with sub-modules: Facilities, Reservations, Calendar, and Maintenance for managing hotel operations.

📄

Document Archiving

Upload, organize, and retrieve important documents. Track file storage usage and view recent uploads.

🧑‍💼

Visitor Management

Register and track visitors, manage check-ins/check-outs, and maintain complete visitor logs.

⚖️

Legal Management

Handle legal documents, compliance tracking, contract monitoring, and regulatory requirements for the hotel.
