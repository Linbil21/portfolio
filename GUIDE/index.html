Dashboard Widgets

1

Compliance Reports

Displays risk categories (High Risk, Recommitted) with completion rates and pending cases count.

2

Recent Activity

Shows latest actions from team members including their roles — Dean Chef, Head Chef, Coordination Officer, and HR Manager.

3

Archiving Status

Displays total files count, storage usage, and recently uploaded files with quick access.

4

Maintenance Schedule

Lists upcoming and past maintenance events with status indicators (Upcoming, Running, Dismissed).
